About Me

Dr. Shrestha holds PhD, MBA, BL degrees and also have CA study background. He has rich professional experience of more than 40 years where he served in senior positions with Government of Nepal, corporate sectors, International Organizations and bilateral agencies.

He has expertise in general management financial management, administration, audit, human resources and training. He trained hundreds of people in share market, investment planning and other trainings. Teaching and training is his passion and he loves to work with youths, especially on financial intelligence.
